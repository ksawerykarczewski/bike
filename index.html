Top 10 rules for cyclists

#01
Keep right at all times
#02
Raise your hand up high before you stop
#03
Give hand signals to the left or right before turning
#04
Watch over your left shoulder before overtaking cyclists
#05
Always overtake other cyclists on the left
#06
Do not ride against the traffic flow or on sidewalks/pavements, pedestrian crossings or pedestrian streets
#07
Cycling is not allowed in parks in central Copenhagen
#08
To turn left at an intersection you must first cross to the opposite right corner of the intersection where you stop and wait for the traffic light to change before continuing
#09
A short ring on the bell is often a signal that a cyclist wants to pass – so please keep to the right
#10
From dusk to dawn, bicycles must be equipped with bicycle lights front and rear
